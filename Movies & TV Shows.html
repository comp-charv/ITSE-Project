Movies & TV Shows

Most people are likely tired of watching the same shows and movies that they have been watching on TV because they are mostly repeated, and some being delayed due to travel restrictions and safety. This has given the rise of streaming services such as HBO Max, Hulu, Netflix, and Disney Plus. Each streaming service has lots of different TV shows and movies that are from the old days, but they came out now; originals are exclusive to certain sites. You only have to pay a monthly or yearly subscription cost to access them. Each service has an expanding library of movies and TV shows that are added every couple of weeks or months. Below are some good shows and movies that you may like.
