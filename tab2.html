Movies & TV Shows

Most people are likely tired of watching the same shows and movies they have been watching on TV since its mostly repeats. This has given the rise of streaming services such as HBO Max, Hulu, Netflix, and Disney +. Each streaming service has lots of different TV shows and movies that are from back in the day, recent came out, or orignals that are exclusive to certain sites. You only have to pay a monthly or yearly subscription cost to access them.
